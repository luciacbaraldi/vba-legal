Primary Legal Services

Immigration & Residency

Legal advice and representation for temporary and permanent residence applications, renewals and changes of status.

Citizenship Proceedings

Professional & Corporate Mobility

Advisory services for companies and professionals relocating to Argentina within regulated legal frameworks.

Regulatory & Administrative Advisory

  • Tax registration (Monotributo) — advisory assistance with enrolment and compliance coordination
  • Company formation — procedural support for incorporation and registrations
  • Bank account opening — coordination with Argentine financial institutions
  • Driver’s licence (car and motorcycle) — recognition, exchange or issuance procedures
  • CUIL / CDI registration — required identifiers for employment and administration
Legal services are provided exclusively by attorneys licensed in the Argentine Republic. Tax, corporate, banking and administrative matters are advisory in nature and may involve coordination with licensed accountants, notaries and third-party professionals. Such services do not constitute legal representation unless expressly agreed in writing.
