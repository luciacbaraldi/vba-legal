The Firm

The firm advises international clients who require clear legal frameworks, documented procedures and predictable interaction with Argentine authorities. Matters are handled with a focus on legal certainty, discretion and continuity.

Legal Services

Immigration and Residency

Legal advice and representation for temporary and permanent residence applications, renewals and changes of status.

Citizenship Proceedings

Judicial filings and procedural strategy for Argentine citizenship based on lawful residence.

Professional and Corporate Mobility

Advisory services for companies and professionals relocating to Argentina within regulated legal frameworks.

Tax Registration (Monotributo)

Advisory assistance with Argentine tax registration, including Monotributo enrolment, category assessment and ongoing compliance coordination.

Company Formation

Advisory support for the incorporation of Argentine legal entities, including initial structuring, registrations and coordination with notaries and accountants.

Bank Account Opening

Procedural guidance and coordination for opening personal and corporate bank accounts with Argentine financial institutions.

Driver’s Licence (Car and Motorcycle)

Assistance with the recognition, exchange or issuance of Argentine driver’s licences for cars and motorcycles.

Legal services are provided exclusively by attorneys licensed in the Argentine Republic. Tax, corporate, banking and administrative matters are provided on an advisory basis and, where applicable, in coordination with licensed accountants, notaries and third-party professionals. Such services do not constitute legal representation unless expressly agreed in writing.
